# Guideline
Webnovel provides a space for authors to freely create stories. 
Our app is publicly available on Google Play and the App Store. To comply with market standards, we have set certain standards for content contributors to ensure that the displayed content maintains the highest quality and adheres to platform guidelines.

This page provides an overview of these standards, our commitment to maintaining them, and the measures we take for content that does not meet our requirements.

# Zero Tolerance
I. In case of any of the following content being detected, immediate removal will be carried out, and we reserve the right to pursue subsequent legal actions:

## Spam and advertisements
Unauthorized or unsolicited commercial promotional content.

## Impersonation of individuals or organizations
Engaging or promoting actions or publicity in a false identity or impersonating others or organizations.

## Intellectual property infringement and plagiarism
Unauthorized use of others' intellectual property, including creative works, brands, or patents; unauthorized copying, imitation, or plagiarism of others' works or ideas.

## Sexual content involving minors
Any sexually explicit content involving minors (under 18). This includes but is not limited to the depiction or implication of minors engaging in sexual activities, sexual abuse, or exploitation.

# Restricted Content 
II. Regarding the following content, we will assess the severity of the plot and the author's subjective attitude towards such behavior. If deemed inappropriate and the author refuses to make modifications, we will take actions such as removing their works:

## Bullying and harassment
Displaying or promoting acts of bullying, harassment, malicious pursuit, or rudeness towards others in the works.

## Hate speech
Discriminatory, hateful, or maliciously attacking speech targeting a specific group or individual. Authors should avoid promoting racism, gender discrimination, religious hatred, etc., in novels.

## Illegal activities
This includes promoting or encouraging any illegal activities such as theft, fraud, drug abuse, etc. Authors should avoid describing actions that violate the law and should not encourage readers to imitate them.

## Promotion of violence
Promoting or glorifying acts of violence, such as brutal brawls, malicious harm, murder, etc. Authors should avoid depicting or excessively glorifying violent scenes.

## Self-harm and glorification of suicide
Promoting or glorifying self-harm or depicting suicide as a beautified or idealized act. Authors should avoid depicting self-harm or romanticizing suicide plots to ensure the health and safety of readers.

## Promotion of abuse
Promoting or glorifying abusive behavior towards others, such as willful harm, animal abuse, etc.

## Involvement of real politics/religion/controversial topics
This refers to directly involving sensitive political, religious, or controversial topics in the works. Authors should be cautious to avoid overly extreme, offensive, or discriminatory descriptions of these topics to prevent controversy or conflict.

# Monitored Content
III. If the following content is involved, we will assess the severity of the plot and the author's subjective attitude. If deemed inappropriate and the author refuses to make modifications, we will restrict the internal and external promotion of their works:

## Explicit or nudity
Please ensure that the content is not excessively explicit, vulgar, or offensive, and avoid involvement in illegal activities, such as rape, incest, etc.
If a work relies heavily on explicit, vulgar, and provocative pornography as its core selling point (ex. rape system), such works will be subject to the following consequences:

1. Removal from the platform once reported by readers.
2. No feature/promotion within the platform.
3. No nomination for future writing contests.
